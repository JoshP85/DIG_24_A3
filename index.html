Getting Here

Plan a trip to Wollongong

Wollongong is located about 70Kms south of Sydney, weather you're travelling by rail or car, there are many options to choose from.

By Rail
  • From Sydney Central Station locate the south coast line which departs every hour.
  • From Sydney International or Domestic terminals locate the T8 Macarthur Via Wolli Creek Line, change at Wolli Creek for the South Coast Line.
  • Travel time from Sydney by rail is approximately 1 hour 40 minutes.
By Car
  • The fastest route is by taking the Princes HWY/A1 and M1 motorways, travel time is around 90mins.
  • A more scenic route is along the Grand Pacific Drive, with great coastals views traveling through the rainforests of the Royal National Park and along the Sea Cliff Bridge.
